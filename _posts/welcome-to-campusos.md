We believe schools deserve better tools.

For too long, campus operations have been held together by duct-taped spreadsheets, disjointed SaaS products, and enterprise software designed for Fortune 500 companies — not for educators.

## The Problem

Most school management software falls into one of two camps:

1. **Enterprise ERP** — Powerful but painful. Implementations take years, cost millions, and require dedicated IT teams.

2. **Point solutions** — Easy to start, but they don't talk to each other. You end up with one tool for scheduling, another for communications, another for facilities, and no single source of truth.

## Our Approach

CampusOS is different. We're building a cohesive operating system for campus management that is:

- **Simple** — Designed for educators, not ERP consultants
- **Integrated** — One platform, one source of truth
- **Open** — Transparent, extensible, no vendor lock-in

## What's Coming

We're starting with the features schools need most: scheduling, resource management, communications, and analytics. And we're building them together with the schools that will use them.
